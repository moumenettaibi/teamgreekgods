FAQ

Find answers to your questions below. If you can't find what you're looking for, feel free to contact us.

01

What is the Team Greek Gods program?

It’s a personalized program that combines training plans, nutrition, and motivation tailored to your specific needs—whether you want to lose weight, gain muscle, or simply improve your fitness. You benefit from continuous support via the TGG FIT app.

02

Is the program suitable for beginners?

Absolutely. The program is designed for both beginners and experienced athletes. Every plan is created based on your current level and your goals.

03

What results can I expect?

Results depend on your commitment, but you can expect a significant physical and mental transformation in 3 months—including fat loss, muscle gain, better health, and increased self-confidence.

04

How is it different from other programs?

24/7 Support: You’re continuously guided by a coach.
Personalization: Every program is tailored to your needs.
Proven Results: Over 9,000 successful transformations since 2018.
Exclusive App: The TGG FIT app offers advanced tracking and tools.
Scientific Approach: Methods validated by nutrition and sports experts.

05

How does the coaching work?

You’ll be in direct contact with your coach for regular adjustments and daily progress tracking. All your questions are answered quickly via WhatsApp.

06

Do I have to join a gym?

No, it’s not mandatory. There are programs adapted for training at home, outdoors, or in a gym—based on your preferences.

07

What equipment do I need to start?

It depends on your program. You can work with basic equipment (dumbbells, resistance bands) or even no equipment at all. If you have gym access, your sessions will be optimized accordingly.

08

What kind of meals are in the nutrition plan?

The plan is based on simple meals tailored to your cultural and dietary preferences. There are also options for specific diets.

09

What if I have dietary restrictions or health issues?

All plans take your restrictions or medical conditions into account. The team works with experts to offer a safe and effective solution for you.
